Standup Siren

A tiny local tray app that plays dramatic music shortly before your daily team meeting.

Download for macOS Download for Linux

No backend. No account. No cloud sludge. Just a tiny local goblin with a theme song.

What it does

Standup Siren sits quietly in your tray and plays a dramatic intro before your meeting starts. Default trigger time is 13:00, with playback starting shortly before it.

How it works

  • Runs locally on your machine
  • Uses a bundled default sound
  • Can use your own ring.mp3 from the config directory
  • Lives in the tray instead of becoming a whole giant app situation

Platforms

  • macOS build available
  • Linux build available
  • Open source under MIT
